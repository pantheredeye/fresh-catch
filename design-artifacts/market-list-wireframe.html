Market List Priority Guide

Priority 1: Status Control
• On/off toggle (immediate) • Active vs paused state • Visual differentiation
Priority 2: Recognition
• Market name (prominent) • Schedule pattern • Next occurrence
Priority 3: Navigation
• Tap to edit settings • Add new market • Section organization
Priority 4: Overview
• Count of active/paused • Reason for pause • Grouping by status
Design Decisions:
  • Toggle is OUTSIDE the tap target (no accidental changes)
  • Next occurrence helps plan upcoming days
  • Paused markets stay visible (not deleted)
  • Compact alternative for power users who know their markets
